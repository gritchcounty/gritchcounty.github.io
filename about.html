ABOUT GRITCH COUNTY WATER AUTHORITY

Gritch County Water Authority was established in 1988 with approval from the Minnesota Pollution Control Agency (MPCA) to manage the Power Creek Water Pollution Control Plant (WPCP) and the Taupe Towers Lane Water Treatment Plant (WTP).
