TITLE: FOR WHEN IT HAPPENS TO YOU

GCWA ARCHIVE ID / FCT: C4149RW290

RUN DATES: 7/23/2004 - 12/17/2004

ALT TEXT: A Blusch ad that reads: "For When It Happens To You." Two green figures converse in the background. In the foreground a distressed green figure holds his head. The ad is produced in collaboration with GCWA, Crake Industrial, and Avlai.

This full-page ad was created for for Blusch for the 10th anniversary of the release of the Marionette 3. In the aftermath of the Superfund disaster at Blusch's manufacturing plant in the Largent township and the subsequent purchase by Mhunck Digital, the digital copies stored at GCWA were deleted at Blusch's request. This copy is a scan from the October 2004 issue of SRM.
