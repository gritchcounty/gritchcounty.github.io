TITLE: I DON'T F*****G LIKE GOOD FOOD NO MORE

GCWA ARCHIVE ID / FCT: C5339R110 / 8CCAP-144.2

RUN DATES: 1/11/2009

The image appears to be a water-damaged scan of a printed document. Text at the top reads: "I don't fucking like good food no more". Below it are three images of dishes which have been crossed out. Text below reads: "And I won't be coming over to eat it! Please stop calling me. Gritch County poisoned our drinking water. I'm sorry."

This classified/personal advertisement was published in the December issue of Skeleton Realm Magazine. It is included in GCWA's archive at the requirement of Condition 29c of the 3/19/2013 Erica Sandison v. Gritch County Water Authority and Mhunck Chemical U.S. Court of Appeals Case No. 08-1023 ruling of the U.S. Court of Appeals Eighth Circuit.
