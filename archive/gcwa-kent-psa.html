TITLE: GCWA KENT & SURROUNDING TOWNSHIPS PSA/DWAN

GCWA ARCHIVE ID / FCT: C5903E404 / 8CCAP-144.1

RUN DATES: 12/2/2008

Kent and surrounding townships are warned that drinking water from the Taupe Towers Lane WTP is unsafe for drinking, washing, cooking, or bathing and should be avoided until further notice.

This notice was published in the December issue of Skeleton Realm Magazine. The acceptable timeframe for publication ended 5/6/2008; however, the CPCA compliance specialist assigned to GCWA's drinking water facility recommended that the notice be published regardless, to comply with the Rules and Regulations.
