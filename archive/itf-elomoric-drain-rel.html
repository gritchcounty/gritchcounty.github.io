TITLE: ELOMORIC DRAIN RELATIONSHIPS AMONG PROXY PLATFORMS

GCWA ARCHIVE ID / FCT: 40DRQW1011 / 8PQOP-202.09

PUBLICATION DATE: 2023-06-07

ALT TEXT: A poorly-preserved scan/photocopy of a document alleging that a group called the Naculates are controlling the world by means of familial language (father, brother, sister, etc.).

This document appears to have been drafted for the 2023 ITF conference in Chevy Chase, MD. The date of the scan is unknown. It is largely illegible but refers to various groups who, it purports, use the media as a tool for population control. This copy is uploaded here at the specific request of the Gritch County Board.
