TITLE: SEE THE WORLD FROM ANYWHERE

GCWA ARCHIVE ID / FCT: A3949308

RUN DATES: 8/11/2019 - 12/5/2022

ALT TEXT: A line art illustration of the Pan-Oculite, partially showing a mossy canyon vista. Text below reads "See the world from anywhere. The Mhunck Digital Pan-Oculite brings the comfort and accessibility of heads-up displays to 2.3 centimeters away. The Pan-Oculite is the multitool of the digital age: binoculars and microscope (72x optical zoom); 60MP camera; monitor with native 1M resolutuion; media computer; and GNU/Linux-based HUD."

The Pan-Oculite needs no introduction. It is manufactured in the Wotton township, and Mhunck Digital supplies the water used in the process from GCWA's Powers Creek WPCP. Klomic, Inc. and Kelloran Imaging have established offices in the Wotton township as well, and GCWA looks forward to continuing to serve them for all their water needs.
