TITLE: HEAL-EM-UPS BY CAPIARY

GCWA ARCHIVE ID / FCT: 9050F1231

RUN DATES: 6/11/1999 — 2/28/2001

The text reads: "You could do it right! Problem: I have 'developed' yellow lesions on my shallow bone parts (hip, wrist, knuckles), and they will not fade or heal with treatment." Ad continues to discuss lesions and avoidance strategies. The solution is to purchase Heal-em-Ups by Capiary and have the small crumpled balls surgically inserted into the skin above the knee.

This poster was created by Capiary in collaboration with GCWA and other industry leaders, most of which still work closely with GCWA. In lieu of payment Capiary offered deeply discounted Heal-em-Ups insertions for employees of the collaborating companies. Due to conflicts of interest GCWA was unable to participate in the barter.

In the years after the ad campaign, Heal-em-Ups were discontinued. They are now considered valuable collectors' items.
