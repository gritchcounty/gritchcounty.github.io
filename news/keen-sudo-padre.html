"KEEN" - SUDO PADRE

The Epoxy Records Club approached GCWA in the autumn of 2023 with an offer to collaborate with one of its instrumental artists, Sudo Padre, which is perhaps best known for its eclectic style and unusual field recordings and musique concr√©te methods. The primary artist responsible for Sudo Padre grew up in Kent, CE and worked in the wastewater industry (including at the Powers Creek WPCP) for several years prior to becoming a musician full-time, and was interested in composing a series of pieces that utilized field recordings made at important places in the water treatment cycle in Kent. With GCWA's permission and accompaniment, the artist visited the Powers Creek WPCP and the Taupe Towers Lane WTP, as well as points of interest in the collection system across Kent and the various lift stations and outfalls that GCWA maintains, and made extensive field recordings at each in different weather conditions.

Using miscellaneous other recordings and methods Sudo Padre creates a calming, gentle reflection of the WPCP and the collection system and treatment process. The album cover will be immediately recognizable to most residents of Kent: the statues are in the Malles Cemetery. The album was incorporated into multimedia outreach programs as background music for GCWA exhibits and showcases. Most recently it was used during the three FOG (Fats, Oils, and Grease) Awareness open houses which GCWA hosted at the Kent Community Center over the summer of 2024.

The album was released to the general public earlier this month on 11/17/2024, and it is available on Bandcamp or streamed on our website.
