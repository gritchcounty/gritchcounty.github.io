GCWA was honored to receive the 12th consecutive Platinum awards for both its Power Creek wastewater treatment plant and Taupe Towers Lane water treatment plant. These awards were given at the 2024 Cebulina Water Professionals Fall Conference. At the conference GCWA was also awarded the Best FOG Outreach Program of 2024 Award.

2024-08-02

A spill of 1,250 gallons of raw sewage was reported at 502 Bybule Avenue, Kent, CE on 8/1/2024 at 17:26. The receiving water was North Easton Pail Creek. The cause was hydraulic loading, a consequence of the 1.7" of rain received over the weekend. The spill was stopped by 19:00, and the area was cleaned and treated with lime. Signs were posted above and below the receiving water site. The incident was reported to CPCA and the Health Department immediately, and a report was published in the August 2024 issue of SRM.
