2024-12-02

A spill of 855 gallons of raw sewage was reported at 909 Tablers Way, Kent, CE on 11/30/2024 at 19:02. The receiving water was North Easton Pail Creek. The cause was vandalism. The spill was stopped at 23:05, and the area was cleaned and treated with lime. Signs were posted above and below the receiving water site. The incident was reported to CPCA and the Health Department immediately, and a notice was published in the December 2024 issue of SRM.

2024-08-02

A spill of 1,250 gallons of raw sewage was reported at 502 Bybule Avenue, Kent, CE on 8/1/2024 at 17:26. The receiving water was North Easton Pail Creek. The cause was hydraulic loading, a consequence of the 1.7" of rain received over the weekend. The spill was stopped by 19:00, and the area was cleaned and treated with lime. Signs were posted above and below the receiving water site. The incident was reported to CPCA and the Health Department immediately, and a notice was published in the August 2024 issue of SRM.

2024-02-23

To commemorate GCWA's thirty-fifth year anniversary, GCWA will be installing a bas-relief bronze plaque at the Kent Community Center. This plaque was designed by community members and chosen in a contest last year, and features the Cebulina state bird, the corncrake, as well as the immediately-recognizable sealstring Barrefour dwellings on the shore of the Martinaque Lake. The unveiling ceremony will be held on Saturday, 3/2/2024 at 11:00 a.m. It will be open to the general public.
